Unlock the Fun: Mastering the Art of Hosting a Teleparty

Do you need to set up a web browser and install the Teleparty extension? Think again! There are a few essential steps to ensure your watch party is flawless. So, don’t wait—gather your 100 closest friends and get ready. Therefore, let’s kick off the ultimate watch party:

  • Get Started with Teleparty To kick off your Teleparty experience, install the Teleparty extension. Ensure you're using a compatible web browser—Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Additionally, you'll need a compatible device like a Windows PC, Chromebook, or MacOS to install the extension successfully. Lastly, click here to install and join the fun!
  • Anchor Your Teleparty Extension To keep Teleparty just a click away, pin the Teleparty extension to your browser’s toolbar. Besides, this ensures the icon appears next to your address bar for quick access. However, if the TP button is missing, click the puzzle icon near the URL bar and add it to your toolbar. This way, the TP button will be conveniently positioned in the top right corner of your screen, primed for use.
  • Access Your Streaming Account While setting up, log into your streaming account by heading to your preferred platform—Disney Plus, Netflix, Hulu, Hotstar, HBO Max, or another service. Moreover, the host and all participants must have subscriptions and accounts to join the watch party.
  • Select, Sync, and Watch There's nothing better than enjoying your favorite videos with friends. First, choose the video you want to watch together. Start playing it, then pause briefly—this is crucial for setting up your watch party.
  • Create Your Teleparty URL This crucial step involves generating a Teleparty link to start your watch party. Click the TP icon located next to your browser’s address bar. This action will open the "Create A Watch Party" window. Click the "Start a Party" button to create a unique watch party link, which you can then share with your friends.
  • Join the Teleparty o join the watch party, click the Teleparty invitation link. Ensure you install the Teleparty extension, even if you’re just a participant. By clicking the link, you’ll be instantly redirected to the watch party, where you can enjoy the movie or show with friends and chat throughout the screening.
